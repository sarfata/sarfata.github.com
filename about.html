Biography

Grown up in France with a passion for computers and the Internet, Thomas was barely an intern during the first Internet bubble. Since graduating, Thomas has enjoyed several entrepreneur life. In the latest, he was CTO of Backelite for six years.

Very curious of all technical things, Thomas has built robots, enjoyed long distance amateur radio, kernel drivers hacking and security testing. When not in front of a computer, he sails, takes pictures or try to understand Yoga.

In 2012, Thomas moved to the bay area for new adventures.

Publications

Programmation iPhone OS3 a book in French on iPhone application programming, was published in october 2009 and was Thomas first technical publication.
